Getting Connected

Now we are ready to get online and start some new adventures! Your NxtGen Console supports mutiple methods in getting your NxtGen experience online. Equipped with the latest wireless technologies simply connect to any wireless network to hit the ground running. Also supported are ethernet and network card compatibility, simply plug into the appropriate ports located on the back of the console.

Minimum network requirements: 3.0 Mbps for localized gameplay. 8.0 Mbps for global gameplay / online play.
